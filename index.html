Our Story

TIPSG (Technology Institute for Professional Skill Growth) was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic education and industry requirements in Kotputli.

As the district's first comprehensive IT training center, we recognized the need for quality technical education that goes beyond traditional classroom learning. Our approach combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical training, ensuring students are job-ready from day one.

Today, we stand proud as a leading institution that has trained over 500 students and placed 300+ professionals in reputed IT companies across India. Our commitment to excellence and student success drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To empower students with industry-relevant skills through world-class training, practical experience, and guaranteed placement opportunities. We aim to create a skilled workforce that drives India's digital transformation.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and sought-after IT training institute in Rajasthan, known for excellence in education, industry partnerships, and student success stories. We envision a future where every Kotputli student has access to world-class IT education.
